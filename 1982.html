1982 Atrocities

Compared to the following years, the number village attacks, massacres, and forced disappearances during the late 1970s and through 1981 were fewer and are more accurately described as a ramp-up to 1982 and 1983. The Guatemalan army began targeting specific areas in the jungles and in the highlands that they viewed as being supportive of the EGP.
